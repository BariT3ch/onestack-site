Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a subscription?

Go to the Subs tab and tap the + button. Enter the name, price, billing cycle, and next payment date.

How does the receipt scanner work?

Go to the Vault tab and tap SCAN. Take a photo of your receipt or select one from your library. Our AI will automatically extract the store name, items, prices, and any warranty information.

How do vehicle maintenance reminders work?

Add a vehicle in the Vehicles tab, then add maintenance items. For oil changes and tire rotations, reminders are based on mileage. For inspections and battery, reminders are based on dates. The app alerts you when service is due.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Your files are stored in private cloud storage accessible only to your account. We never sell or share your data.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to Profile and contact us to request account deletion. All your data will be permanently removed.
